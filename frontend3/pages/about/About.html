About Our Askadmit Chatbot

How It Works

Our chatbot is designed to understand and respond to a wide range of questions. Here's how you can interact with it:

  1. Start by typing your question in the chat window.
  2. The chatbot will respond with relevant information instantly.
  3. If needed, the chatbot can direct you to further resources or connect you to a live assistant.
